Athens & Beyond

Getting There & Around

Getting In

Fly into Athens International Airport (ATH). Taxi (~€40 flat rate, ~40 min), metro Line 3 to Syntagma Square (~40 min), or private transfer. If you’re heading straight to an island, many have direct international flights — worth checking before defaulting to ATH.

🚕 Taxi ~€40 🚇 Metro ~40 min 📱 Uber / FreeNow

Getting Around

Athens is very walkable in the center. FreeNow has the best local driver availability. The metro is clean, cheap, and efficient. Google Maps works great for transit.

Where to Stay

We recommend Plaka (charming village feel, narrow streets) and Thiseio (laid-back, beautiful Acropolis views from the promenade). Both are centrally located.

Things to Do in Athens

Ancient history and modern energy sit right on top of each other.

Acropolis ↗
The must-see. Go early to beat crowds and heat. Parthenon, Erechtheion, and Temple of Athena Nike.
Acropolis Museum ↗
World-class collection at the base of the hill. Top-floor gallery with Parthenon views is unforgettable.
National Garden ↗
Leafy escape next to the Parliament building. Great for a break from the sun.
Vrachakia ↗
Lesser-known rocky area with a local feel. Worth exploring off the beaten path.
Philopappos Monument ↗
Best panoramic views of the Acropolis and city. Especially beautiful at sunset.
Odeon of Herodes Atticus ↗
Ancient Roman theatre built into the Acropolis slope, still used for summer performances. Catch a concert or show if the season lines up — it's unforgettable.
🗓 Suggested Order
Day 1
  • Acropolis — early morning
  • Acropolis Museum
  • Plaka stroll & dinner with a view
Day 2
  • National Garden & Parliament
  • Philopappos Hill at sunset
  • Vrachakia if you’re feeling adventurous
⏰ Timing Tips
  • Book Acropolis tickets online in advance
  • Go before 9 AM or after 5 PM to beat the heat
  • Arrive 30 min before sunset for Philopappos
  • Acropolis Museum closed Tuesdays — check ahead

Beyond Athens

Island Escapes

Each island has its own personality.

Mykonos

Glamorous, loud, and unapologetically fun

Beach clubs, DJs, late nights, and beautiful people. But old town Chora is genuinely charming, and there’s a real archaeological gem nearby.

Beaches

Good but below average by Greek island standards. You’re here for the scene. Paradise and Super Paradise are more party than swim.

Don’t Miss

Delos ↗
Short boat ride — one of Greece’s most important archaeological sites. Birthplace of Apollo and Artemis. Visit with a guide.
The Windmills ↗
Iconic row overlooking the harbor. Classic photo spot, beautiful at sunset.

Eat & Drink

🐦
Pelican ↗
Nick’s top pick for a meal on the island.
🥂
Caprice ↗
Sunset drinks on the water in Little Venice. Stunning views.
🎉
Skandinavian ↗
The go-to club for a big night out.
🌊 Essentials
  • Stay in old town Chora, not beach hotels
  • Visit Delos — don’t skip it
  • Sunset drinks: Caprice in Little Venice
  • Big night out: Skandinavian
  • Best meal: Pelican
🚢 Getting There
  • Ferry from Piraeus: ~2.5–5 hrs
  • Flight: ~45 min from ATH
  • Book at ferryscanner.com

Santorini

Dramatic cliffs, legendary sunsets, real depth

Built on a volcanic caldera — cliffs into deep blue water, white buildings stacked together. Romantic, photogenic, historically rich.

Where to Stay

Fira is lively. Oia is fancier with the iconic sunset. Stay along the caldera coast between them for a middle ground.

Things to Do

Oia Castle (Agios Nikolaos) ↗
15th-century Venetian fortress ruins. The sunset spot — arrive early.
Fira to Oia Hike ↗
~10 km caldera rim trail. Very little shade — go early, bring water.
Akrotiri ↗
Bronze Age settlement — the “Pompeii of the Aegean.” Over 3,500 years old.
Ancient Thira ↗
Hellenistic ruins on a ridge between Kamari and Perissa beaches.
Museums in Fira
Museum of Prehistoric Thera & Archaeological Museum of Thera. Pair with Akrotiri.
Red Beach & White Beach
Dramatic colored cliffs. Red Beach on foot, White Beach by boat.
Ammoudi Bay ↗
Hidden harbor below Oia. Hike or donkey down for swimming and seafood.

Eat & Drink

🥙
Yogi Gyro ↗
In Fira — best gyros on the island.
🌅
PK Cocktail Bar ↗
The spot for sunset drinks.
🌅 Essentials
  • Stay in Oia for iconic sunset views
  • Book caldera-view hotels early
  • Hike Fira → Oia (10 km, go early)
  • Visit Akrotiri archaeological site
  • Sunset drinks: PK Cocktail Bar
  • Best gyros: Yogi Gyro in Fira
🚢 Getting There
  • Ferry from Piraeus: ~8 hrs (overnight)
  • High-speed ferry: ~5 hrs
  • Flight: ~45 min from ATH

Crete

The largest island — feels like its own country

Gorges, beaches, Venetian harbors, ancient palaces, incredible food. Plan at least a full week.

Getting There

✈️ Chania (CHQ) ✈️ Heraklion (HER) ⛴ Overnight ferry

Where to Stay

Chania — Lively harbor town. Try Riviera Suites. Elounda — Relaxed, upscale northeast coast.

Things to Do

Samariá Gorge ↗
~16 km, one of Europe’s longest. Full day. Book a tour bus from Chania.
Elafonisi Beach ↗
Pink-sand lagoon. Almost tropical. Dedicate a full day.
Palace of Knossos ↗
Center of Minoan civilization, home of the labyrinth. Get a guide.
Heraklion Archaeological Museum ↗
Finest Minoan collection in the world.
Fortezza (Rethymno) ↗
Venetian fortress. Stop for a visit and lunch between Heraklion and Chania.
Firka Fortress (Chania) ↗
At Chania’s old harbor entrance. Great views.
Spinalonga ↗
Fortified island near Elounda. Short boat trip to explore.

Eat & Drink

🥐
Red Jane Bakery ↗
In Chania — don’t skip it.
🍸
Sinagogi ↗
Best bar in Chania.

We’ve barely scratched the surface. Do your own research — or better yet, ask a local.

🏔 Essentials
  • Plan at least a full week
  • Base in Chania — try Riviera Suites
  • Book Samariá Gorge tour the day before
  • Red Jane Bakery — don’t skip it
  • Best bar in Chania: Sinagogi
🚢 Getting There
  • Fly to Chania (CHQ) or Heraklion (HER)
  • Overnight ferry from Piraeus — get a cabin

Paros & Naxos

Close neighbors, easy to visit together

Ferries between them take 30–45 minutes. Either is great on its own.

Naxos

The most well-rounded island — beaches, history, mountain villages, great food, youthful energy.

Guided bus tour
Hit the island’s major sights with a local guide.
Catamaran cruise
Swimming in secluded coves with lunch on board.
Portara (Temple of Apollo) ↗
Iconic marble gateway. Beautiful at sunset.

Paros & Antiparos

Paros has charming Parikia and pretty Naoussa. Antiparos has a great secluded cliff beach hike. Ask around when you arrive.

✨ Naxos Essentials
  • Best all-around Cycladic island
  • Book a guided bus tour
  • Catamaran cruise: coves & lunch on board
  • Portara at sunset — unmissable
  • Paros: Parikia & Naoussa village
🚢 Getting There
  • Ferry from Piraeus: ~5 hrs to Naxos
  • Paros ↔ Naxos: 30–45 min
  • Book at ferryscanner.com

Other Islands Worth Knowing

Milos

Volcanic coastline, sea caves, colorful beaches. Sarakiniko’s white lunar landscape is iconic. Quieter, rugged — great for couples.

Rhodes

Medieval Old Town is a UNESCO site — a walled city for hours. Beaches, ancient Lindos, different vibe from the Cyclades.

Corfu

Northwest, closer to Italy. Venetian influence, lush green hills, elegant architecture around Corfu Old Town. Longer journey but completely different.
